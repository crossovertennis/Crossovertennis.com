ABOUT LANCE

Built at the Pinnacle

Lance C. Johnson is an elite tennis coach and mental performance specialist with over 30 years at every level of the game. A UC Berkeley history graduate and former ITF professional, Lance was training alongside Dmitri Tursunov (top-20 ATP) when Richard Williams found him, saw his potential, and brought him into the Williams family program.

He became a hitting partner and on-court resource during Venus and Serena Williams' rise to World #1 and #2 one of the first male hitting partners on the WTA side. Later engaged by Brad Gilbert to develop top professionals. Mentored by Jose Higueras. Involved with the production of the Serena Williams MasterClass.
